# Bonsai Marketplace - Product Vision

A marketplace connecting Bonsai enthusiasts in South Africa - enabling buying, selling, and community knowledge sharing.

## The Problem

Bonsai enthusiasts in South Africa lack a dedicated platform to:
- Buy and sell specimens safely
- Connect with other enthusiasts
- Get expert advice and build knowledge
- Trust sellers/buyers in transactions

## The Solution

**Bonsai** (working name) - A two-sided marketplace with community features.

## Core Value Propositions

### For Sellers (Individuals)
- List personal collections for sale
- Build reputation through ratings
- Reach targeted audience of enthusiasts

### For Sellers (Businesses)
- Nurseries and garden shops can list inventory
- Professional presence in the market
- Access to verified buyers

### For Buyers
- Discover quality specimens
- Trust through seller ratings
- Expert community for advice

### 1. Marketplace Core
- User registration (individual + business accounts)

- Buyer/seller transaction flow

# User Personas

Primary user archetypes for the Bonsai marketplace.

## Persona 1: The Collector (Johan)

### Demographics
- Age: 45-60
- Location: Johannesburg suburbs
- Occupation: Professional (engineer, accountant, etc.)
- Income: Upper-middle class

### Bonsai Journey
- 10+ years experience
- Member of local bonsai club
- Collection of 20+ trees
- Occasionally sells/trades specimens

### Goals
- Find rare species
- Connect with other serious collectors
- Sell trees that don't fit collection
- Share knowledge with community

### Pain Points
- Hard to find quality trees online
- No way to verify seller reputation
- Facebook groups too chaotic
- Shipping concerns for valuable trees

### Feature Priorities
1. Advanced search/filtering
2. Seller verification
3. High-quality photo uploads
4. Secure messaging

---

## Persona 2: The Hobbyist (Thandi)

### Demographics
- Age: 28-40
- Location: Cape Town
- Occupation: Creative professional
- Income: Middle class

### Bonsai Journey
- 1-3 years experience
- Learning from YouTube and forums
- Owns 3-5 trees
- Wants to expand collection affordably

### Goals
- Find affordable starter trees
- Learn care techniques
- Connect with local sellers
- Get advice from experienced growers

### Pain Points
- Intimidated by serious collectors
- Unsure what to pay for trees
- Wants to see trees before buying
- Needs guidance on species selection

### Feature Priorities
1. Price filtering
2. Community forum for questions
3. Seller ratings/reviews
4. Location-based search

---

## Persona 3: The Nursery Owner (Pieter)

### Demographics
- Age: 35-55
- Location: Pretoria area
- Business: Small nursery/garden center
- Bonsai is 20% of inventory

### Goals
- Reach bonsai-specific audience
- Move inventory efficiently
- Build reputation as bonsai source
- Reduce marketing costs

### Pain Points
- Facebook ads expensive, low conversion
- General marketplaces not targeted
- Bonsai buyers hard to reach
- No way to showcase expertise

### Feature Priorities
1. Business seller profile
2. Bulk listing management
3. Verification/trust badge
4. Analytics on listing performance

---

## Persona 4: The Casual Browser (Lerato)

### Demographics
- Age: 25-35
- Location: Durban
- Occupation: Various
- Bonsai interest: Curious/beginner

### Goals
- Learn if bonsai is for them
- Find entry-level trees
- Understand pricing
- Get inspired by community

### Pain Points
- Overwhelmed by options
- Don't know where to start
- Worried about killing trees
- Limited budget

### Feature Priorities
1. Browse without account
2. Beginner-friendly forum categories
3. Price transparency
4. Care information

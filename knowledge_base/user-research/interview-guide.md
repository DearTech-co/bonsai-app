# User Interview Guide

Structured approach for conducting user research interviews with bonsai enthusiasts.

## Interview Goals

1. Validate persona assumptions
2. Understand buying/selling pain points
3. Gauge willingness to pay
4. Identify feature priorities
5. Discover unmet needs

## Pre-Interview Checklist

- [ ] Schedule 30-45 minute session
- [ ] Send calendar invite with video link
- [ ] Prepare recording consent form
- [ ] Review participant's background (if known)
- [ ] Test recording equipment

## Interview Structure

### Opening (5 minutes)

**Introduction:**
- Thank participant for their time
- Explain purpose: understanding bonsai community needs
- Get consent for recording
- Assure confidentiality

**Warm-up questions:**
- "Tell me about yourself and your interest in bonsai"
- "How long have you been involved with bonsai?"

### Background (5-10 minutes)

**Experience level:**
- "How would you describe your bonsai experience level?"
- "How many trees do you currently have?"
- "Are you part of any bonsai clubs or communities?"

**Current behavior:**
- "Where do you currently buy bonsai?"
- "Have you ever sold a bonsai? If so, where?"
- "What online platforms do you use for bonsai-related activities?"

### Pain Points (10 minutes)

**Buying experience:**
- "Tell me about the last time you bought a bonsai online"
- "What was frustrating about that experience?"
- "What information do you need before buying a tree?"
- "How do you decide if a seller is trustworthy?"

**Selling experience (if applicable):**
- "Tell me about the last time you sold a bonsai"
- "What challenges did you face?"
- "How did you find buyers?"

### Feature Exploration (10 minutes)

**Show concept/prototype if available:**
- "What's your first impression?"
- "What would you want to do first?"
- "What's confusing or missing?"

**Feature priorities:**
- "If you could only have 3 features, what would they be?"
- "How important is a community forum to you?"
- "Would you use messaging to contact sellers?"

### Willingness to Pay (5 minutes)

- "Would you pay for a premium listing that gets more visibility?"
- "What would you expect to pay for [feature X]?"
- "Would a monthly subscription make sense for you?"
- "What would make you NOT use a paid feature?"

### Closing (5 minutes)

- "Is there anything else you'd like to share?"
- "What would make you excited to use a bonsai marketplace?"
- "Can we follow up if we have more questions?"
- Thank participant and explain next steps

## Post-Interview Tasks

- [ ] Review recording within 24 hours
- [ ] Create summary in feedback-synthesis.md
- [ ] Tag insights by persona type
- [ ] Identify quotes for stakeholder presentation
- [ ] Update personas if new patterns emerge

## Question Bank by Persona

### For Collectors (Johan persona)

- "Tell me about your most prized tree"
- "What makes a tree worth collecting?"
- "How do you find rare species?"
- "What would you pay for a verified seller badge?"

### For Hobbyists (Thandi persona)

- "What's the biggest challenge as someone learning bonsai?"
- "Where do you go for advice when something goes wrong?"
- "How do you decide what price is fair?"
- "What would help you feel confident buying online?"

### For Nurseries (Pieter persona)

- "What percentage of your sales come from bonsai?"
- "How do you currently market to bonsai enthusiasts?"
- "What would make a platform worth paying for?"
- "What reporting/analytics would be useful?"

### For Casual Browsers (Lerato persona)

- "What first got you interested in bonsai?"
- "What's stopping you from buying your first tree?"
- "What questions do you have that you can't find answers to?"
- "What would make a platform welcoming to beginners?"

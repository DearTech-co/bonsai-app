# Market Analysis

Research on the South African bonsai market and competitive landscape.

## Market Overview

### South African Bonsai Community
- Active bonsai clubs in major cities (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria)
- Growing hobbyist interest driven by social media
- Mix of collectors, growers, and casual enthusiasts

### Current Market Channels
- Local bonsai clubs and shows
- Facebook groups and marketplace
- Gumtree classifieds
- Nursery sales (limited bonsai-specific)
- International imports (expensive)

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
Currently no dedicated bonsai marketplace in South Africa.

### Indirect Competitors

| Platform | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| Facebook Groups | Large audience, free | No trust system, poor search |
| Gumtree | Established, general marketplace | Not bonsai-specific, spam issues |
| Local Nurseries | Physical inspection | Limited selection, geography |

## Market Opportunity

### Gap Analysis
- No dedicated platform for SA bonsai community
- Existing channels lack trust/verification
- Forum + marketplace combination unique
- Mobile-first approach underserved

### Target Segments
1. **Collectors** - Buying/selling premium specimens
2. **Hobbyists** - Starter trees, supplies, advice
3. **Nurseries** - Business accounts, bulk listings
4. **Clubs** - Event promotion, member sales

# Product Vision

Bonsai is a marketplace for Bonsai enthusiasts in South Africa, connecting collectors, nurseries, and hobbyists.

## Mission

To create the premier destination for buying, selling, and discussing bonsai in South Africa, fostering a trusted community of enthusiasts.

## Core Value Propositions

### For Sellers
- **Individual collectors**: Easily list and sell bonsai from personal collections
- **Business sellers**: Nurseries and garden shops reach targeted audience
- **Trust building**: Verification and ratings establish credibility

### For Buyers
- **Discovery**: Find unique bonsai across South Africa
- **Trust**: Verified sellers and bidirectional ratings
- **Community**: Forum for advice and knowledge sharing

## Key Differentiators

1. **South Africa Focus** - Local marketplace with SA-specific features (provinces, cities, ZAR currency)
2. **Bidirectional Trust** - Both buyers AND sellers are rated
3. **Community Integration** - Forum built into the marketplace experience
4. **Verified Sellers** - Business verification for professional sellers

## Product Pillars

### 1. Marketplace
- Listing discovery with advanced filtering
- Seller profiles with trust scores
- In-app messaging for buyer-seller communication
- Purchase history tracking

### 2. Trust System
- Bidirectional ratings (buyer and seller)
- Seller verification badges
- Trust score calculation
- Abuse prevention

### 3. Community Forum
- Category-based discussions
- Q&A with accepted answers
- Expert knowledge sharing
- Moderation system

### 4. User Experience
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast, modern React frontend
- Real-time notifications
- Seamless authentication
